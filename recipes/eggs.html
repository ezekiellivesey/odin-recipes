Eggs, spinach and tomatoes

A breakfast that will fuel you throughout the day

Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs
  • 150g Spinach
  • 150g Cherry tomatoes
  • 2 Slices of toast
  • 2 Tbsp unsalted butter

Steps

  1. Turn a pan on to medium
  2. While waiting for the pan to heat up slice cherry tomatoes in half
  3. Butter the pan then add spinach and tomatoes
  4. Once they have softened, scoop them out onto a plate
  5. Add more butter to the pan
  6. Crack all 3 eggs into pan and wait for the bottom to cook
  7. Add a lid once the bottom has cooked so that the top of the eggs can cook
  8. Place 2 slices of bread in the toaster while waiting
  9. Once the eggs have cooked, season them with whatever you like and place them onto the plate
  10. Grab the toast and eat!
